Our Team

Time to learn a little about our team

Aahan Jain

Aahan is a chai-loving free spirit who enjoys long drives accompanied by music. Ever the explorer, he never spares a chance to try out something
new and loves to learn about different cultures. Being a people person, he’d love to get to know you and share anecdotes. Having been an Ideation
Manager for E-cell IITH, Content and Curation Coord for TEDxIITHyderabad, Sponsorship Manager for Elan&Nvision IITH, he’s a great team player.
He brings the same energy to his work as Overall Head. If you ever want to spend a whole night awake just to watch the sunrise, he’ll be delighted to
join you

Nikhil P

Nikhil P is tech-savvy and is deeply fond of legacy and retro-tech items! He is a 3rd-year student.
Pursuing a BTech in Electrical Engineering at IIT Hyderabad. Working with circuits and gadgets boosts his enthusiasm, so does gaming!
He is a coordinator at the Esports and Gaming club of IIT H, Glitch!
Interning at Intel, he sure does express his love for electronics in his work.
He brings his 'A-Game' to Marketing and Sales as a core at TEDxIIT Hyderabad.

Shruti Chepuri

Shruti takes every opportunity to explore new things and grow. She is a district-level volleyball player and has been selected for the national-level baseball team. She is also a classical dancer and enjoys meeting new people. Being a foodie, she enjoys cooking. In her time at IITH, she has held many positions of responsibility. Some of them are the Placement Coordinator at the Office of Career Services, Student Welfare Secretary in the Student Gymkhana at IITH, and of course, the Finance and Treasury Core at TEDxIITHyderabad.

Akansha Singh

Akanksha is a designer passionate about creative concepts that solve a problem. Being naturally social, she thrives in a collaborative environment. She brings to the table a fresh approach, meticulous hard work and a fun time filled with stories. She draws her inspiration from everyday life, observing everything that catches her eye. When she’s not designing, she’s found mostly binging on shows or some Shah Rukh Khan movie for the 100th time. In general, she’s perky and talkative, but that’s only after she has had her cup of morning coffee.

Arya Rajendran

Arya is a post-graduate student in Development Studies at IITH and is highly passionate about writing, acting and speaking. Extremely fond of jeeps and bikes, she can't get enough of new books, bullet journals, and shoes! She comes with the warning of 'A whole lot of drama is what you are going to encounter!' Trying to find her way out of the box, and ending up in the larger sphere, as usual, is all a matter of perspectives according to her, and she owes it all to 'Unlearning. 'Note: She can go all the way from hyperactive to nerveless in 0.00001 seconds, and you might want to keep that in mind!

Reethu Sanagala

Reethu Sanagala is an active listener and people-oriented person who loves to work and deal with people to bring profitable outcomes with affirmative conversations. She is currently pursuing BTech 3rd year from the CSE department at IIITR. In 2020, she worked as a business developer intern in Village Agro, and is presently working as a full-stack developer at an IITH startup named Sabz. She is presently the overall head of the Training and Placement Cell and the Marketing & PR lead in the Google Developers Student club of IIITR.
